In short

  • ✓ Can: There're some things your allowed to do:
    • Sublicense: This software allows you to give partial production/distribution rights to third parties not included in the license.
    • Modify: This software can be modified.
    • Commerical Use: This software can be used for commercial purposes in derivative works.
    • Distribute: The software may be distributed.
  • ✗ Cannot: Software is released without warranty and the software/license owner cannot be charged for damages.
  • (!) Must: You must retain the original copyright notice.
